Frequently asked questions
How does PandaRoc work day to day?

The Finder scans your ROC accounts' pending list (Εκκρεμείς) on a schedule and adds new orders to Pending. You decide which orders to accept, and the Downloader collects the certificates as soon as ROC generates them, filing them straight into your download folder.

Why isn't the Finder doing anything right now?

It runs during business hours (Mon–Fri 08:00–19:00 by default — change the schedule in Settings → Advanced) and stays quiet outside them on purpose, so your accounts don't look like a machine to ROC. It also pauses while you're over your plan's account limit or while ROC is temporarily blocking access.

What does "Waiting for ROC" mean?

The order is accepted but ROC hasn't generated its certificates yet. That's normal and needs nothing from you — the Downloader re-checks every cycle and downloads automatically once they're ready.

What does "Needs attention" mean?

ROC reported something only a person can resolve: a name application was rejected, an order was returned for corrections, or an accepted order is no longer visible in the workspace. Open the order to see the exact reason.

Where are my certificates saved?

In the folder you chose under Settings → Download folder — organised into a subfolder per order, or flat if you prefer. The files stay on your machine (or network drive); nothing is uploaded to us.

What counts against my plan's accounts?

Enabled ROC accounts. Disabling an account frees its slot immediately; to add more slots, change your plan from your account page at pandaroc.com — the app picks the change up automatically within a few minutes.

ROC blocked the connection — what now?

PandaRoc detects it, pauses both agents and waits a couple of hours before resuming on its own — pushing through a block only makes it longer. Avoid VPNs or proxies; they make blocking worse, not better.

Are my ROC passwords safe?

They never leave this computer. They're stored encrypted on your machine and used only to log in to the ROC portal locally — our servers handle just your PandaRoc account and subscription.

How accurate is Name Check?

It scores a proposed company name against the Registrar's naming rules and a live search of the registry. Treat it as a strong, honest estimate — the final decision always belongs to the Registrar.

How do updates work?

The app checks for new versions on startup and shows a banner when one is available — one click installs it. You can also check manually under Settings → Updates.
