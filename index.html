What it does

You decide what to accept. We handle the rest.

PandaRoc watches your ROC accounts around the clock. When new orders come in, you choose which ones to process, and the agents take over from there.

Finder

Opens a browser for each of your ROC accounts and stays on standby. When a new order appears it lands in your queue. Accept it and the Downloader takes over.

Always watching Multi-account Runs in background

Downloader

Checks whether your documents are ready to download, saves certificates into the right folder and renames them. It also tracks whether a name has been approved or rejected and flags any returned orders.

Auto-resume Smart filing Status tracking

Trello sync

Every accepted order becomes a Trello card. Labels, custom fields and client names sync both ways, every five minutes and instantly when you make a decision.

Who we are

Built where the paperwork is.

PandaRoc was built inside a working corporate services firm, not a software startup. PandaServe LTD has handled company formations, annual filings and compliance in Cyprus since 2010, which means the ROC portal is part of our daily routine.

PandaRoc began in 2025 as an in-house fix. Andreas built it so his father's team could stop logging into ROC accounts one by one each morning, hunting for pending orders and downloading certificates by hand. Other firms had the same problem, so we turned it into a product. It runs on your own machine, keeps your credentials encrypted and local, and works through your queue with the kind of patience that never runs out.
