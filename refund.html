Refund & Cancellation

How cancellations and refunds work, including our 14-day money-back guarantee.

Last updated: 8 June 2026

14-day money-back guarantee

We want you to be happy with PandaRoc. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work. It is offered by PandaServe LTD (reg. no. HE 317541).

0114-day money-back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with PandaRoc, email us within 14 days of a charge and we will refund that payment in full, no questions asked.

02How to request a refund

Email hello@pandaserve.eu from the email address on your account, mentioning the charge date and amount. We aim to process eligible refunds within a few business days.

03Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel any time from Manage billing on your account page. When you cancel, your subscription will not renew, and you keep access until the end of the period you have already paid for.

04Free trial

You are not charged during the 7-day free trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be billed at all.

05How refunds are issued

Refunds are returned to your original payment method through our payment provider, Stripe. Depending on your bank, it may take a few additional days to appear on your statement.

06Contact

Questions about billing or refunds? Email hello@pandaserve.eu.
