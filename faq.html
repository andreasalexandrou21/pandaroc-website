Help & FAQ

Common questions about activation, billing, and using PandaRoc. Can't find your answer? Email us below.

What is PandaRoc?

PandaRoc is a desktop app that automates downloading company registration certificates from the Cyprus Registrar of Companies (ROC / DRCOR) eFiling portal. It watches your pending services, downloads certificates as they're issued, and keeps everything organised — so you don't have to log in and check by hand.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

You get full access to every feature for 7 days. A card is required to start, but you're not charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before then at no cost. If you cancel during the trial, you simply won't be billed.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing is per ROC account, per month. Pick how many accounts you need:

Starter — 1 account — €29 · Small Firm — 5 accounts — €99 · Professional — 10 accounts — €179 · Firm — 20 accounts — €299.

You can choose any number from 1 to 20 on the pricing page. Need more than 20? Contact us for Enterprise.

What counts as a "ROC account slot"?

One slot = one ROC eFiling login that PandaRoc manages for you. If your firm files under three separate ROC accounts, you'd want a 3-account plan. The app lets you add up to the number of accounts your subscription covers.

How do I activate the app after subscribing?

Three steps:

1. Download and install PandaRoc for Windows or macOS.
2. Open the app and click the gear icon (top-right), then choose License.
3. Paste your license key and click Activate.

Where do I find my license key?

It's shown on the confirmation page right after you subscribe, and it's always available on your account page once you're logged in. The key looks like PR-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX.

Can I change how many accounts I'm paying for?

Yes. Go to your account page and click Manage Billing to open the secure billing portal, where you can increase or decrease your account count at any time. Your price adjusts accordingly.

How do I cancel, and what's your refund policy?

You can cancel anytime from Manage Billing on your account page — your access continues until the end of the period you've paid for.

We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee: if PandaRoc isn't right for you, email us within 14 days of a payment and we'll refund it in full.

Is my ROC login information secure?

Yes. Your ROC credentials and all automation run locally on your own computer — they're never sent to our servers. Our license server only handles your PandaRoc account and subscription, nothing to do with the registry itself.

Which operating systems are supported?

Windows 10 and 11 (64-bit), and macOS 12 or later (Apple Silicon and Intel).

The app won't activate or says my license is invalid — what should I do?

First, check your internet connection and make sure you copied the full key exactly from your account page. If it still won't activate, email us with your account email and we'll sort it out quickly.
